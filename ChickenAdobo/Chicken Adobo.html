How To Make Chicken Adobo

What is Chicken Adobo?

Chicken Adobo is the most popular Filipino dish. The word "Adobo" is a spanish word meaning "marinade".
Many Filipino households have their own version of this popular recipe, but this recipe will focus on the most popular version of all.

What Will I Need?

You are going to need the following ingredients in the kitchen:
  • Cooking Oil
  • Garlic
  • Whole Peppercorns
  • Bay Leaves
  • Soy Sauce
  • White Vinegar
  • Chicken
  • White Rice
  • Water

You are going to need to use the following in the kitchen to cook the recipe:
  • Stove
  • Rice Cooker
  • Pan
  • Tongs
  • Cutting Board
  • knife
  • Measuring Cup
  • Bowl and utensils for serving

Instructions:

  1. Add Rice in Rice Cooker.
  2. Add Water and set Rice Cooker.
  3. Heat up Oil in Pan.
  4. Smash up and dice the Garlic.
  5. Add Diced Garlic into Hot Pan with Oil
  6. When Garlic is turning slightly brown, add the Chicken into the Pan.
  7. As the Chicken is cooking, Add Bay Leaves and Whole Peppercorns.
  8. Once Chicken is cooked to a medium rare, add the Soy Sauce and Vinegar ad stir till content is mixed.
  9. Cover Pan with lid and stir occassionally.
  10. When the Rice and Chicken are cooked, Add rice to the bowl and top the rice with the Chicken. Marinade the chicken on the rice with the sauce from the pan.
  11. Eat with a smile!
