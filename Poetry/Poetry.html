Poetry of Me

From The Mind of Philippe Recto


The Seed

Deep beneath me lays a seed that craves for water. To be bathed a kinetic force from within.
I seek the need that nature requires. To fullfill a promise to reach its peak. To reach and to break free with color and pain.
To struggle for more as we feed the hunger. To take energy from light and air. To feel the blossom that shows my nature.
To wilter and to faulter. To go back from wence you came. To make room for another. So we can repeat this garden again.

Vicious Love

A ruthless show of affection with her gnashing teeth, she aims to impress her boy. A sigh echoes
in the chambers they dwell, dancing on three legs at a time. To viciously scorn her captured prey.
To sleep in the arms of her life. "I shall impress again tomorrow" she claims, without hesitation or snide.

Dancing Dots

From one on to another, they move just like no other, a pattern that moves with your mind.
A wand of precision, and a headdress to quicken. a curly line to stop you on time. steps on the lines
will be filled with my lines and repeated till they come to the bridge.
As far as it goes, the silence aproach, and bows to the end of the line.

Say Something

Say, speak, show, touch. A movement, a brow lightens. a grimace of dispair or shame.
As all things crumble and shatter. As time stood still in her pain. Sorry, what?, sorry, why? An exchange of discomfort.
A shiver strangles the pride and ruffles the fear. A glimpse into a reality of hurt.
A trust beyond its repair. Cry. Leave. Gone. Something.

Bursting at the Seams

You want it to implode so you can make a move. A slow ripping silence builds.
It breaks and burns and so you learn and become the new born from inside.
An interest practiced, chance of new breathe, an opportunity to succeed and become.
A dream i once had of being complete. A window of freedom, my home.
